ABOUT THE WEBSITE
This website allows you to search various information about Pokémon. You can search sets of Pokemon species by using the filters Generation, Game Version or Favourites: all the pokemon species will be displayed on the left. The results are ordered following the natural pokedex order. By clicking on a Pokémon species, the information about the said species will be displayed in the card on the right. Each Pokémon is charaterized by its pokédex number its category, one or two types, its generation, its weight and height, a set of descriptions and its statistics (health points, attack, defense, special attack, special defense, speed). Some species possess alternative forms, visible under the Varieties label. By clicking one of them, you can display the information about that alternative form.
On the right, under the Pokémon image, there are three symbols:
  • Arrow: click on it to watch the pokémon from the front and the back;
  • Star: click on it to see the shiny version of the pokémon, in it's alternative colors;
  • Heart: click on it to add that pokémon species to your favourite pokémon list, it will remain saved even if you close the tab.
